Vision & Mission

VISION:
“Jain senior secondary school aspires to be a nationally acclaimed leader in education in all spheres which transforms the life of students through integration of teaching and character building.”

MISSION:
To contribute to the development of students by synthesizing teaching and creative activities. To provide an enviable environment and state-of-the art technological exposure to its scholars. To develop human potential to its fullest extent and make them emerge as world class leaders in their professions and enthuse them towards their social responsibilities.


About Tijara

Tijara is a city and a municipality in Alwar district of the Indian state of Rajasthan. Tiara comes under the NCR area and situated 48 km to the northeast of Alwar.
